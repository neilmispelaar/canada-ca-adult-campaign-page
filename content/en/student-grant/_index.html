Canada Student Grant Expanded Eligibility

From Employment and Social Development

Going back to school is now more affordable

Innovation is changing how we live and work. You can now afford to go back to school to upgrade your skills or even pursue a new career path.

  • $1,600 per year in grants

    Returning to school on a full-time basis after more than 10 years in the workforce? Get an extra $1,600 in grants each school year.

  • Grants determined by your current income

    Big change in your financial situation? Now your eligibility for the Canada Student Grant is based on your current income.

Apply for the Canada Student Grant that’s right for you

Full-Time Students

A grant for low- and middle-income students enrolled in a full-time undergraduate program at a designated post-secondary institution.

Full-time Students with Dependants

A grant for full-time students with dependent children and assessed financial need.

Part-Time Students

A grant for low- and middle-income students with a financial need who are studying part-time at a designated post-secondary institution.

Part-Time Students with Dependants

A grant for part-time students who qualify for a Canada Student Loan and have dependent children.

Students with Permanent Disabilities

A grant for students with a permanent disability enrolled at a designated post-secondary institution.

Services and Equipment for Students with Permanent Disabilities

A grant for students with a permanent disability to help pay for education-related costs.
